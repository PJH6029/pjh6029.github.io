Research Focus

Modeling future-ready AI systems

I explore data, architecture, and tooling decisions that shape how large language models reason, interact, and improve themselves. Current work emphasizes API-grounded agents beyond standard ReAct and scalable evaluation of agentic behaviors.

Agent architectures

Designing tool-call orchestration that composes planners, memory, and execution monitors to better solve complex multi-stage tasks.

Training pipelines

Investigating pre- and post-training workflows (LoRA, RLHF/shaping, distillation) that boost sample efficiency without sacrificing alignment.

Interpretability & multimodality

Building insight into how language-first systems can reliably reason across perception channels with transparent intermediate states.
